Garden Maintenance Course

Fees: R750

Purpose: To provide practical knowledge of garden maintenance, including proper watering, pruning, and planting techniques.

Content:

  • Water Restrictions and Watering Requirements: Understanding water needs for indigenous and exotic plants.
  • Pruning and Propagation of Plants: Techniques for maintaining healthy and attractive plants.
  • Planting Techniques for Different Plant Types: Proper planting methods to ensure growth and sustainability.

Benefits:

Participants will be equipped to maintain beautiful, sustainable gardens, which can lead to career opportunities in garden maintenance services.

Course Description

This course equips participants with the skills to maintain gardens, care for plants, prune shrubs, and ensure a thriving garden environment.
