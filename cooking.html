Cooking Course

Fees: R750

Purpose: To provide the knowledge and skills necessary to prepare nutritious family meals, helping individuals and families improve their dietary habits.

Content:

  • Nutritional Requirements for a Healthy Body: Learning about essential nutrients and balanced diets.
  • Types of Protein, Carbohydrates, and Vegetables: Understanding food groups and their benefits.
  • Planning Meals: Creating well-balanced meal plans.
  • Preparation and Cooking of Meals: Practical cooking techniques for daily meals.

Benefits:

Graduates will have the ability to prepare wholesome meals, contributing to a healthier lifestyle for themselves and their families.

Course Description

Our Cooking course is designed for beginners and experts alike, offering a wide range of culinary skills to create delicious meals.
