First Aid Course

Fees: R1,500

Purpose: To equip individuals with first aid awareness and basic life support skills for emergency situations, ensuring they can respond effectively to injuries or medical crises.

Content:

  • Wounds and Bleeding: Identifying types of wounds and managing bleeding effectively.
  • Burns and Fractures: Learning how to treat burns, fractures, and other traumatic injuries.
  • Emergency Scene Management: Understanding the protocols to manage an emergency scene safely.
  • Cardio-Pulmonary Resuscitation (CPR): Hands-on training on CPR techniques for life-saving response.
  • Respiratory Distress: Recognizing and managing cases of choking and airway blockage.

Benefits:

Participants will gain the confidence and skills necessary to provide life-saving care during emergencies, making them valuable in workplaces, homes, and communities.

Course Description

This course teaches the essential skills of first aid and emergency response, preparing participants to handle critical situations effectively.
