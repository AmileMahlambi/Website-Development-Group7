Sewing Course

Fees: R1,500

Purpose: To provide individuals with sewing skills for garment alterations and the creation of new designs, equipping them to work in tailoring and design services.

Content:

  • Types of Stitches: Learning various stitches for different types of fabrics and purposes.
  • Threading a Sewing Machine: Basics of setting up and operating a sewing machine efficiently.
  • Sewing Buttons, Zips, Hems, and Seams: Practical knowledge on garment finishing techniques.
  • Alterations: Skills to make clothing adjustments for fit and style.
  • Designing and Sewing New Garments: Techniques for creating customized clothing pieces.

Benefits:

Participants can enter the fashion industry or start their own small tailoring business, providing customized tailoring services to clients.

Course Description

Learn the art of sewing, from beginner techniques to advanced fashion design, in our hands-on course.
