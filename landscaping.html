Landscaping Course

Fees: R1,500

Purpose: To provide essential skills in landscape design and maintenance for both new and established gardens, preparing participants for a career in landscaping services.

Content:

  • Indigenous and Exotic Plants and Trees: Identifying and selecting appropriate plants for different environments.
  • Fixed Structures: Understanding the placement of fountains, statues, benches, tables, and built-in braai setups.
  • Balancing of Plants and Trees: Achieving aesthetic balance and harmony in garden layouts.
  • Aesthetics of Plant Shapes and Colors: Creating visually appealing gardens through plant selection.
  • Garden Layout: Designing practical and beautiful garden spaces.

Benefits:

Graduates will have the knowledge to start their own landscaping business or work as professional landscapers, creating and maintaining gardens for clients.

Course Description

Learn landscaping techniques, from garden design to sustainable maintenance practices, in our comprehensive course.
