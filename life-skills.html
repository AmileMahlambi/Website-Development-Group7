Life Skills Course

Fees: R1,500

Purpose: To equip individuals with essential skills for handling daily life challenges, enhancing their personal independence and confidence.

Content:

  • Opening a Bank Account: Understanding banking basics and financial independence.
  • Basic Labour Law: Know your rights as an employee and employer.
  • Basic Reading and Writing Literacy: Improving communication and comprehension skills.
  • Basic Numeric Literacy: Developing essential math skills for personal finance.

Benefits:

Graduates will gain fundamental life skills, increasing their self-sufficiency and preparing them for better employment and social opportunities.

Course Description

This course equips learners with the skills needed for effective communication, personal development, and time management.
